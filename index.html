    A Letter From My Heart

    ❤
    April 30, 2026
    With All My Love, Your Child

    Dearest Mom and Dad,

    As I sit down to write this, words feel too small to capture what I truly feel. Twenty years. Two decades of unwavering love, patience, and sacrifice. I cannot imagine a world where you two aren't together, because your love has been the very foundation of everything good in my life.

    Mom, you are the heartbeat of our home. Your warmth, your endless prayers, your gentle guidance, and the way you always put everyone before yourself has taught me what true love looks like. Every meal you've cooked with love, every story you've told me, every worry you've carried silently - all of it has shaped who I am today.

    Dad, you are the strength that holds us together. Your hard work, your wisdom, your quiet dedication, and the way you always make sure our family has everything we need has shown me what it means to be a protector and a provider. Every lesson you've taught me, every time you've been there for me, every sacrifice you've made without complaint - has made me the person I am today.

    But most importantly, watching the two of you together has been the greatest lesson in love I've ever received. The way you still smile at each other after all these years, the way you support each other through thick and thin, the way you've built a home filled with love, laughter, and memories - this is what I aspire to have one day.

    Twenty years is not just a number. It's 7,300 days of choosing each other. It's thousands of moments of forgiveness, understanding, and unconditional love. It's a lifetime of memories that I have been blessed to witness and be a part of.

    Thank you for being the most amazing parents anyone could ask for. Thank you for loving each other so that I could learn what love truly means. Thank you for being my role models, my guides, my safe place.

    This website is a small token of my love for you two. I hope it brings a smile to your faces, just as you have brought countless smiles to mine.

    I love you both more than words can ever express. Here's to 20 years of beautiful togetherness, and to many, many more years of love, laughter, and happiness.

    Happy 20th Anniversary, with all my love,

    Jash
