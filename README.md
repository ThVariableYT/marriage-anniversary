## &#128240; Adding Your Photos

To replace the photo placeholders with actual photos of Mom and Dad:

1. **Prepare your photos** - Collect beautiful images of your parents (wedding, family, vacation, etc.)

2. **Add photos to the repository:**
   - Go to the repository
   - Click "Add file" > "Upload files"
   - Upload your photo files (recommended names: `photo1.jpg`, `photo2.jpg`, etc.)

3. **Update index.html:**
   Find the `<div class="photo-placeholder ...">` sections in the `index.html` file and replace them with:
   ```html
   <img src="photo1.jpg" alt="Your photo description" class="photo-image">
   ```

4. **Recommended photo sizes:**
   - Width: 800-1200px
   - Height: 600-900px  
   - Format: JPG or PNG
   - File size: Keep under 2MB for faster loading

## &#127926; Changing the Music

To change the background music:

1. Upload your audio file (MP3 format) to the repository
2. In `index.html`, find the `<audio id="bg-music">` section
3. Update the `src` attribute to point to your file:
   ```html
   <source src="your-music-file.mp3" type="audio/mpeg">
   ```
